Popular Social Media Platforms

Facebook

Facebook is a social networking platform where users can share updates, photos, and connect with friends and family. It's one of the largest social media platforms worldwide.

Twitter

Twitter allows users to post short messages called "tweets" to share news, opinions, and interact with others in real-time. It's popular for real-time updates and news.

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing app where users can post images, short videos, and stories. It is highly popular among influencers and brands for visual content.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where users can build their professional profiles, connect with peers, and apply for jobs. It's the largest platform for professional networking.
